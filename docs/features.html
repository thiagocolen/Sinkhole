Key Features

Sinkhole is designed with a focus on reliability, security, and user control. Here are the standout features that make it the ideal bridge for your mobile data.

Free Obsidian Sync Alternative

Obsidian users on Android often struggle with cloud synchronization. Sinkhole provides a robust solution:

  • Seamless Bridge: Connects Android's local storage to Google Drive, bypassing traditional SAF limitations for third-party apps.
  • Full Vault Sync: Mirrors everything—including your configuration, plugins, and themes.
  • Cross-Platform Harmony: Sync to Google Drive and access your vault on Desktop via the Google Drive client.

Intelligent Synchronization

Our sync engine is built for efficiency:

  • Manifest-Based: Uses .sync-manifest.json to track state and detect changes without expensive hashing of every file.
  • Delta Sync: Only transfers what has actually changed, saving your battery and mobile data.
  • Conflict Resolution: Employs a "Last Write Wins" (LWW) strategy to ensure your most recent work is always preserved.

Modern User Experience

A tool that works hard should also look good:

  • Theme Support: Beautiful Light, Dark, and System-adaptive themes.
  • Responsive UI: Optimized for phones, tablets, and foldable devices.
  • Real-time Logs: Full transparency with detailed sync metrics and status updates.
