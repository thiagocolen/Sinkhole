Getting Started

Ready to bridge your data? Follow this comprehensive user guide to get Sinkhole up and running on your Android device.

  • 1

    Installation

    Download and install the Sinkhole APK from our releases page. You may need to enable "Unknown Sources" in your device settings to complete the installation.

  • 2

    Launch and Permissions

    Open the app and grant the initial folder permission. Choose the specific folder on your device that you wish to synchronize (e.g., your Obsidian vault).

  • 3

    Google Authentication

    Authenticate with your Google account. A dedicated folder called SinkholeFolder will be automatically created in your Google Drive.

  • 4

    Perform Initial Sync

    Click the SYNC NOW button. Sinkhole will analyze your folder, generate a local manifest, and upload your files to the cloud.

  • 5

    Verify in the Cloud

    Open your Google Drive on any device to verify that your files have been successfully uploaded and are ready for cross-platform access.

Pro Tip

Once your data is on Google Drive, use the **Google Drive Desktop** application to mount your drive as a local disk. You can then point your desktop applications (like Obsidian) to the `SinkholeFolder` for a complete sync cycle!
